Who it's for

If your team needs a base, not a lease

CTO has been hosting teams and project crews since 2011. These are the types of teams that work well here.

Small companies and startups
Two to eight people who need to sit together, have a proper address, and want meeting rooms and a podcast studio without the overhead of their own premises. Month-to-month means you scale up or down as the business does.
Hybrid teams with a Cape Town contingent
Part of your team is remote, part is in Cape Town. A dedicated pod gives the local team a consistent base without the cost of a full office for the whole company.
Film and TV production teams
Pre-production, production and post-production all have different space requirements. CTO provides a functional, connected base in the CBD for production coordinators, art departments, location scouts and editorial teams -- for as long as the project runs.
Tech and creative project crews
Sprint teams, agency project crews, consultants on a fixed engagement. You need a real office for three months, not a co-working drop-in. CTO gives you a dedicated space without the commitment of a lease that outlasts the project.
Film & TV production

Cape Town is one of the world's leading film destinations. Your production needs a base.

Cape Town has hosted major international productions for decades -- from Hollywood features to premium streaming series. The infrastructure is here. The crews are here. What's often missing is a functional, connected office base in the CBD for the teams running the production on the ground.

CTO is three blocks from the De Waterkant production hub and minutes from the Cape Town CBD film permitting offices. Cape Town International Airport is 20 minutes away -- straightforward access for international crew arriving on location. Fast fibre, meeting rooms for production meetings, phone booths for sensitive calls, a podcast studio for recorded briefings, and 24/7 access for teams working across time zones.

We work project to project. Your desk setup matches your production schedule -- in for pre-production, scaled up through the shoot, wound down for post. No lease that outlasts the wrap party.

Production office
A proper connected base for production coordinators and producers. Fast internet, meeting rooms, printing and 24/7 access.
Art department
Space to spread out, plan and coordinate. Desk space, meeting rooms for briefings, and a quiet environment for detailed prep work.
Location and logistics
A CBD base with easy access to the city, suburbs and surrounding areas. Close to film permitting, municipal offices and key suppliers.
Post-production editorial
Quiet, reliable, fast. 500Mbps fibre for heavy file transfers with dedicated LAN/Ethernet at each workstation. Phone booths for confidential client reviews.

How it works

Tell us about your project. We'll work out the rest.

Every team and project is different. The length of your engagement, the number of desks, whether some team members need full-time access and others need to drop in occasionally -- these are the things that shape the right setup for you.

Rather than a fixed pricing table, we work it out together. Drop Lizelle a line with a bit of detail about your team -- how many people, what you're working on, and roughly how long you need the space. She'll come back to you quickly with what makes sense.

No deposit. No lock-in. We work project to project, which means your setup can scale up and down as the project demands, and end when the project ends.
