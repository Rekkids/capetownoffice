What's on

Four things worth staying for

Predominantly for members, but open to all. If you can see it on the calendar, you're welcome to RSVP.

Lunch Club
Monthly · Usually 1st Tuesday or Wednesday

We pick a spot in the neighbourhood, someone makes a reservation, and anyone who wants to join comes along. No agenda, no structure. Just lunch and good conversation with people you work alongside.

AI Show & Tell
Monthly · Dates voted on in WhatsApp

Members share what they're building, testing or breaking with AI. No slides required. Demos, experiments, things that worked, things that didn't. Plans for the next session are discussed and voted on in the group.

Vinyl & Wine
Every two months · RSVP required · BYOB

A listening session in the space. Records on the turntable, wine in hand, good company. Small and intimate -- 30 to 40 people max. RSVP is required so we know who's coming. BYOB.

RSVP below · BYOB
The CTO Hang
After hours · Format varies

Could be a record, could be a documentary, could be drinks and a chat. The format changes but the idea stays the same -- stick around after work, wait out the traffic, be somewhere good with people you like.
