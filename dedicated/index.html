How it works

One month at a time. Renew if it works, walk away if it doesn't.

Most coworking spaces present 6 and 12-month terms first, with month-to-month as the expensive afterthought. At CTO there is no long-term option -- not because we don't want you to stay, but because we'd rather you stay because it works.

Pay for a month. Come in whenever you like, 24 hours a day. Your desk is yours -- set it up the way you want it, leave your monitors, keep your things. No packing up at the end of the day.

At 3 or more office days a week, a dedicated desk works out more economical than any Flexi package -- and removes the hour-counting entirely.

Who it's for

If you need more than a day, less than a lease

The full-time remote worker
Your company doesn't have a Cape Town office. You need a real desk, not a kitchen table. A dedicated desk gives you a professional address and a permanent workspace without a lease.
The small business
Two to four people who need to sit together, have meeting space available, and want a proper office without the overhead of their own premises. The team pod is built for this.
The contractor between projects
You work intensively for a few months, then take a break. Month-to-month means you pay for what you use and step away when a project wraps, without breaking a lease.
The hybrid team
Part of your team is remote, part is in Cape Town. A dedicated pod means the Cape Town contingent has a consistent base -- without the cost of a full office for the whole company.
