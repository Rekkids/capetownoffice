Ways to work from CTO

Not everyone on your team works the same way -- and your membership doesn't have to be one-size-fits-all. Some team members come in once or twice a week and suit a Flexi membership. Others are here every day and need a dedicated desk. You can blend, mix and match, scale up when a project demands it and scale back when it doesn't.

Not sure where to start? Book a free viewing and we'll work it out together -- or drop Lizelle a line at info@capetownoffice.com.
