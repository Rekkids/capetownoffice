Why it's here

A proper studio. Part of the space.

Most coworking spaces treat a podcast studio as an upsell -- a premium room you pay per hour to access. At CTO it's just part of the space. Every membership includes full use of the studio. Book it the same way you'd book a meeting room -- online, no approval needed.

It was built for members who were already recording -- the podcast, the interviews, the client calls that needed proper audio. The equipment is professional, the room is acoustically treated, and it's available around the clock.

If you've never used it before, come in for a viewing and we'll show you around. It takes about five minutes to get comfortable with the setup.

The setup

Professional equipment. No compromise.

Everything in the studio is production-grade. You bring the content, we provide the tools.

RØDECaster Pro
The industry-standard podcast production console. Multi-track recording, built-in processing, USB audio interface. Everything you need in one unit.
RØDE Microphones
Professional condenser microphones for up to four guests or hosts. Broadcast-quality audio straight out of the box, no setup expertise required.
Acoustic treatment
The room is acoustically treated to eliminate echo and background noise. What you record sounds like a studio, not a spare room.
How to book

Three steps. Done.

1
Check you're a member
The studio is included in all memberships -- Flexi, Dedicated, and Teams. Day pass guests get 1 hour included. Not a member yet? Book a viewing first.
2
Book online
Use the booking link to reserve your time. If you've booked and no longer need it, please cancel so someone else can use it.
3
Come in and record
First time? Check with Lizelle or Andri before your session to make sure you know your way around the equipment. After that, it's yours.

Who uses it

More than just podcasters

Podcasters and content creators
The obvious one. Professional equipment, acoustic treatment, no per-hour charge. Record your show, interview your guests, produce your content.
Coaches and consultants
Client sessions, online workshops, recorded training content. Professional audio makes a noticeable difference when your voice is your product.
Remote teams on calls
When the open floor is too noisy and a phone booth is too small. The studio fits up to four people and the audio quality is noticeably better than a laptop microphone.
Anyone who needs to sound good
Job interviews, investor calls, recorded presentations, webinars. The studio is available anytime -- you don't have to be a podcaster to use it.
